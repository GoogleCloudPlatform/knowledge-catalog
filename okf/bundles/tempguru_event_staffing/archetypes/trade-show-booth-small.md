# Trade Show / Booth Activation, Small

> **Status: draft.** Scope and role mix below are confirmed; specific numeric
> defaults (anchor headcounts, lead-time bands, shift hours) are still under
> review and are intentionally omitted here rather than guessed.

## Scope

One to three exhibitor booths at a trade show, exposition, or industry conference.
The client is the **exhibitor**, not the show organizer. The driver is
brand-forward representation at the booth: engage attendees, demo products,
capture leads. Typically 1–2 show days; setup and teardown are billed separately.

## Default role mix (shape, not rates)

- **[Brand Ambassadors](../roles/brand-ambassadors.md)**, the primary booth role; scales with booth count.
- **[Booth Monitors](../roles/booth-monitors.md)**, optional; add for high-value demo equipment or overflow coverage.
- **[Setup & Breakdown](../roles/setup-breakdown.md)**, per-event; separate shifts from show-floor staff.
- **[Team Leads](../roles/team-leads.md)**, auto-added by the planner when any single shift reaches 20 staff.

Rates for every role come from [pricing](../pricing/rate-matrix.md), keyed to the
event city's [market tier](../pricing/market-tiers.md).

## Compliance flags

- **California:** TempGuru's W-2 model means no AB5 exposure for the client, call it out proactively. See [CA profile](../compliance/by-state/ca.md).
- **New York:** check [wage-notice and spread-of-hours rules](../compliance/by-state/ny.md).

## Cancellation

Cancellations within 24 hours of a scheduled shift start incur a fee equal to
(headcount on that shift × 4 hours × the per-position hourly rate) for that shift
only. See [quote submission](../workflows/quote-submission.md).
