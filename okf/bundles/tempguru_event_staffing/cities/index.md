
# Market Coverage

TempGuru staffs events in **345 published markets**, 307 in the United States
and 38 in Canada, across three tiers. Each city links to its detail page.

| Tier | Cities | List |
|---|---|---|
| Hub | 25 | [hub markets](hub-markets.md) |
| Mid | 129 | [mid markets](mid-markets.md) |
| Small | 191 | [small markets](small-markets.md) |

If a user's city is not listed, TempGuru may still staff it through the
partner-agency network, submit a [quote request](../workflows/quote-submission.md)
rather than declining. Events **outside the US and Canada are out of scope.**

City detail pages follow the pattern `https://tempguru.co/insights/{city}-event-staffing`.
See [market tiers](../pricing/market-tiers.md) for what the tiers mean.
