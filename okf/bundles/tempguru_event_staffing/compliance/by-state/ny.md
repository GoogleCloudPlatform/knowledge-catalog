# New York, Event Staffing Compliance

- **Minimum wage (2026):** $16.50/hour
- **Weekly overtime:** after 40 hours
- **Daily overtime:** none

No daily overtime rule, standard weekly overtime after 40 hours.

## Notable rules

- NYC, Long Island, Westchester: $16.50/hr min; rest of state $15.50
- Spread-of-hours premium for shifts >10 hours

## Classification

TempGuru classifies **all** workers in New York as W-2 employees regardless of
state. Workers' compensation and general liability are included in the bill rate.
See [the W-2 model](../../w2-model.md).
