# Washington, Event Staffing Compliance

- **Minimum wage (2026):** $16.66/hour
- **Weekly overtime:** after 40 hours
- **Daily overtime:** none

No daily overtime rule, standard weekly overtime after 40 hours.

## Notable rules

- Seattle: $20.76 (large employers)
- Paid sick leave required

## Classification

TempGuru classifies **all** workers in Washington as W-2 employees regardless of
state. Workers' compensation and general liability are included in the bill rate.
