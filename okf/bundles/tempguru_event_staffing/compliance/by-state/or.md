# Oregon, Event Staffing Compliance

- **Minimum wage (2026):** $14.70/hour
- **Weekly overtime:** after 40 hours
- **Daily overtime:** none

No daily overtime rule, standard weekly overtime after 40 hours.

## Notable rules

- Portland Metro: $15.95, non-urban: $13.70

## Classification

TempGuru classifies **all** workers in Oregon as W-2 employees regardless of
state. Workers' compensation and general liability are included in the bill rate.
See [the W-2 model](../../w2-model.md).
