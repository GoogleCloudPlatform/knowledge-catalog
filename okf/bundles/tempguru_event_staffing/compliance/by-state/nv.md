# Nevada, Event Staffing Compliance

- **Minimum wage (2026):** $12.00/hour
- **Weekly overtime:** after 40 hours
- **Daily overtime:** after 8 hours

Daily overtime after **8 hours**.

## Notable rules

- Daily overtime after 8 hours for employees earning less than 1.5x min wage

## Classification

TempGuru classifies **all** workers in Nevada as W-2 employees regardless of
state. Workers' compensation and general liability are included in the bill rate.
