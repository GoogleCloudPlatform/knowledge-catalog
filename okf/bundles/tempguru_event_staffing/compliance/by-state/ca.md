# California, Event Staffing Compliance

- **Minimum wage (2026):** $16.50/hour
- **Weekly overtime:** after 40 hours
- **Daily overtime:** after 8 hours

Daily overtime after **8 hours**.

## Notable rules

- Daily overtime after 8 hours
- Double-time after 12 hours/day
- Mandatory meal break by 5th hour
- 10-min paid rest break per 4 hours
- Seventh-day overtime

## Classification

TempGuru classifies **all** workers in California as W-2 employees regardless of
state. Workers' compensation and general liability are included in the bill rate.
