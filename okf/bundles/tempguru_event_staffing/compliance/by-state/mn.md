# Minnesota, Event Staffing Compliance

- **Minimum wage (2026):** $11.13/hour
- **Weekly overtime:** after 48 hours
- **Daily overtime:** none

No daily overtime rule, standard weekly overtime after 48 hours.

## Notable rules

- Overtime threshold 48 hours/week for small employers

## Classification

TempGuru classifies **all** workers in Minnesota as W-2 employees regardless of
state. Workers' compensation and general liability are included in the bill rate.
